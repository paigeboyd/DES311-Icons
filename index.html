Hallgrimskirkja, Reykjavik

A Lutheran Parish church in Reykjavik, Iceland. At 73 metres it stands as Iceland's largest church and the sixth tallest architectural structure.

Trevi Fountain, Rome

A fountain in the Trevi district in Rome, Italy. Standing 26.3 metres high and 49.15 metres wide, it is the largest Baroque fountain in the city and one of the most famous fountains in the world.

Halászbástya, Budapest

The Halászbástya, or Fisherman's Bastion, is a terrace in neo-Gothic and neo-Romanesque style. It is situated on the Buda bank of the Danube, on the Castle hill in Budapest, around Matthias Church.
